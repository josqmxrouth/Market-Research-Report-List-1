<p><p>The aspiration and biopsy needle market includes three main types: Fine Needle Aspiration Biopsy (FNAB), which uses a thin needle to extract small tissue samples; Core Needle Biopsy (CNB), which utilizes a larger needle to obtain cylindrical samples, providing more tissue for analysis; and Vacuum Assisted Biopsy (VAB), which employs a vacuum mechanism to enhance tissue retrieval, allowing for larger samples with minimal invasiveness. Each method caters to different diagnostic needs and tissue characteristics in various medical contexts.</p></p>

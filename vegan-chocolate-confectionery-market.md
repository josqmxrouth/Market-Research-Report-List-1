<p><p>The vegan chocolate confectionery market is categorized into three main types: milk chocolate, dark chocolate, and white chocolate. Vegan milk chocolate mimics traditional milk chocolate using plant-based milks or nut creams, offering a creamy texture without dairy. Dark chocolate, typically higher in cocoa content, appeals to purists seeking rich flavor and health benefits, often containing less sugar. White chocolate, although traditionally sugar-based with no cocoa solids, is recreated in vegan formats using cocoa butter, sweeteners, and plant-based ingredients, providing a sweet, creamy alternative.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Medicine Packaging Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-free Adhesive</li><li>Waterborne Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medicine packaging adhesive market consists of three main types: solvent adhesives, solvent-free adhesives, and waterborne adhesives. Solvent adhesives contain volatile organic compounds, providing strong bonding but raising environmental concerns. Solvent-free adhesives eliminate these solvents, offering a more eco-friendly option with similar adhesion properties. Waterborne adhesives use water as the primary solvent, minimizing VOC emissions and enhancing safety. Each type serves distinct applications in pharmaceutical packaging, balancing performance, regulatory compliance, and environmental impact.</p></p>

<p><p>Watermark packaging services encompass two primary types: visible and invisible watermarks. Visible watermarks are overtly placed designs or patterns that deter counterfeiting and signal authenticity; they enhance brand recognition while remaining apparent to consumers. In contrast, invisible watermarks employ advanced technology to embed watermarking features undetectably within the packaging. This type provides an added layer of security by allowing manufacturers to verify authenticity without visible indicators, appealing to businesses prioritizing discreet brand protection and anti-counterfeiting measures.</p></p>

<p><p>Paclitaxel-eluting stents are specialized medical devices used to treat narrowed or blocked coronary arteries. These stents are coated with paclitaxel, a chemotherapeutic agent that inhibits cell proliferation, thereby preventing restenosis (re-narrowing of the artery) after angioplasty. The unique mechanism of action allows these stents to reduce the risk of complications associated with traditional bare-metal stents.</p><p>The Paclitaxel-eluting Stent Market is experiencing notable growth, driven by a rising prevalence of coronary artery diseases, increasing patient awareness, and advancements in stent technology. As healthcare providers shift towards minimally invasive procedures, the demand for these stents has surged.

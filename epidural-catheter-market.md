<p><p>The epidural catheter market is primarily divided into two types: continuous epidural catheters and single-dose epidural catheters. Continuous epidural catheters are designed for prolonged pain management, allowing for the steady administration of anesthetic or analgesic agents over time. In contrast, single-dose epidural catheters are used for specific, short-term procedures, providing a one-time dose of medication to manage pain. Both types are essential in various medical settings, particularly in labor and delivery, postoperative care, and chronic pain management.</p></p>

<p><p>The surgical endoscope for the neurosurgical market comprises two primary types: rigid neuroendoscopes and flexible neuroendoscopes. Rigid neuroendoscopes feature a fixed design, allowing for precise visualization and minimal invasiveness during procedures like tumor resections or hydrocephalus treatment. They provide excellent optical clarity and stability. In contrast, flexible neuroendoscopes are more adaptable, enabling access to complex anatomical areas. Their design allows for maneuverability within tight spaces, making them suitable for intricate neurological interventions such as biopsies or disease exploration.</p></p>

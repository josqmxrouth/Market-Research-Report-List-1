<p><p>The biopharma buffer market consists of various types, including phosphates, acetates, TRIS, and others. Phosphate buffers maintain pH stability in cellular processes, while acetate buffers are favored for their low ionic strength, preventing protein precipitation. TRIS buffers are widely used for their effective pH range in biochemical applications. Other buffer types may include citrates and glycines, catering to specific needs in bioprocessing and diagnostics. Together, these buffers ensure optimal conditions for biopharmaceutical development and production.</p></p>

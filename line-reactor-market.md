<p><p>The line reactor market is categorized into two main types: AC line reactors and DC line reactors. AC line reactors are used to improve power quality and reduce harmonic distortion in alternating current systems, enhancing the performance of electrical equipment. In contrast, DC line reactors are employed in direct current applications, helping to stabilize the current and protect against voltage spikes. Both types play crucial roles in industrial settings, ensuring efficient operation and extending the lifespan of electrical systems.</p></p>

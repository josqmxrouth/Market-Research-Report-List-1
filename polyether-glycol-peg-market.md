<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyether Glycol (PEG) is categorized into two main market types: Type I and Type II. Type I PEG includes low-molecular-weight variants primarily used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and industrial applications due to their solubility and non-toxicity. Type II PEG consists of high-molecular-weight forms utilized in applications such as lubricants, surfaces coatings, and bio-therapeutics, offering enhanced properties like viscosity control. Both types are essential in various industries, meeting diverse requirements and performance standards, driving their unique market dynamics.</p></p>

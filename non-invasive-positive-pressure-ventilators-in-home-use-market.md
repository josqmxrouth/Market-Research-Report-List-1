<p><p>Non-invasive positive pressure ventilators for home use are classified into two main types: single-level and bilevel ventilators. Single-level ventilators deliver a constant pressure to assist patients in breathing, typically suitable for those with mild respiratory conditions. Bilevel ventilators, on the other hand, provide two pressure settings: a higher pressure for inhalation and a lower pressure for exhalation, making them ideal for patients with varying respiratory needs, such as those with obstructive sleep apnea or chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD).</p></p>

<p><p>The biopesticides market comprises three main types: microbial pesticides, plant pesticides, and biochemical pesticides. Microbial pesticides use natural microorganisms to control pests, promoting environmental safety and sustainability. Plant pesticides are derived from plants and exploit their natural defenses to repel or kill pests. Biochemical pesticides consist of naturally occurring substances that disrupt pest behavior or growth without harming beneficial organisms. Together, these categories provide eco-friendly alternatives to traditional chemical pesticides, supporting sustainable agriculture practices.</p></p>
